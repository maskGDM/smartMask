About the Smart Face Mask

Masks are recommended as a barrier that helps prevent respiratory droplets from traveling through the air to other people when they cough, sneeze, speak, or raise their voices. However, due to the high demand during the pandemic, it has been shown that people make their masks at home, this can give a false sense of security. Choosing the wrong mask lowers the level of protection required against inhalation of infectious agents.

Masks are particularly essential in times where social distancing is difficult to maintain. But correct and prolonged use, for example in clinical settings, causes contamination by the user himself, creating a false sense of security since people are unaware when the toxic gases outside or inside the mask reach dangerous levels for health.
